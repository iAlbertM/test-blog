Becoming a web developer

Hello World!
My name is Albert Martinez. Love what I do everyday, I get to help people everyday being a problem solver... I am a Web Developer. Proud to be coding for fun and for work. All just to keep up with the speed of technological advancements. There is so much good to come from learning self-discipline and good time management skills.

You have to do it for yourself but that doesn't mean you have to do it by yourself. Stay calm and keep coding whilst knowing the developer community is a crowd of ingenious minds and helpful people. The catch... you have to know to ask for help when you need it. Be you and follow your heart. if you have questions, get stuck, and/or need a point in the right direction: Send me a message. I'll respond as soon as I can. Good Luck & see you out in the World Wide Web.
