Becoming a web developer

Hello World!
My name is Albert Martinez. I am a Web Developer and love what I do. I get to help people everyday by doing something else I enjoy, solvng problems. There is so much work that goes into my everyday work but learning self-discipline and good time management skills is key.

You have to do it for yourself but that doesn't mean you have to do it by yourself. When issues arise and feel stuck, stay calm because the developer community are a helpful bunch willing to help you when you need it. The catch... you have to know to ask. If you have questions or need a point in the right direction, send me a message. I'll respond as soon as I can. Good Luck & see you out in the World Wide Web.
